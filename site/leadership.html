Leadership

Satinder Bhalla

CEO

Satinder is the president at TransSight leading business strategy and operations. Always looking for partners who are client driven and delivery focused. He brings in over 17 years of experience delivering solutions and services in the area of consulting services, traveler information systems, transit fare collection, and tolling.

Before starting on his dream journey, Satinder have been in the leadership roles with number of transportation and software leading companies. He enjoys working with clients, stakeholders and end-users, and known for delivering challenging projects.

Satinder lives in SF Bay Area with his family and enjoys hiking and playing volleyball. He continues to be passionate about cricket.

Bimesh Giri

VP, Product and Services

Bimesh Giri is the head of engineering and operations at TransSight where he is responsible for spearheading all technology initiatives across the company. With a focus on innovation and creativity, Bimesh Giri has successfully delivered numerous products and solutions during his professional career.

Before co-founding TransSight, Bimesh Giri has served in various technical leadership roles leading a geographically distributed development team. He has passion for writing code and has been developing applications since early 2000.

Bimesh lives in Bay Area with his family and enjoys biking with his wife and daughter during weekends. He has a Masters in Computer Science from CSU Northridge.
