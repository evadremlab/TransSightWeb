About Us

TransSight(TS) brings over 100 years of collective experience working on transportation projects that range from Advance Traveler Information System (ATIS), 511, Tolling and Transit Fare Collection. TransSight is a leader in technology development, integration, and program management that has successfully accomplished major Intelligent Transportation System (ITS) projects. We have successfully delivered solutions and services for over 80+ government organization worldwide. Our goal is to provide proactive support, on-time and within budget project delivery with upmost project quality.

Mission Statement

TransSight offers transportation organizations innovative services and solutions meeting their internal business and user needs and/or challenges. By connecting things and automating processes that includes integrating systems and data sources to provide a holistic picture, offering analytical and dashboard engine to allow continual improvements and by key focus on end-user experience.

Our Vision

To be a recognized as a valuable and innovative partner for the businesses, government and their users that we serve. We will strive to deliver innovative services and scalable solutions for our customers.
